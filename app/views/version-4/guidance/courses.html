What courses are available

There are four different types of NPQ, depending on the stage of your teaching career.

National Professional Qualification for Middle Leadership (NPQML)

This is designed to enable candidates to develop the knowledge, skills and confidence required to lead high-performing teams and improve classroom practice

National Professional Qualification for Senior Leadership (NPQSL)

This provides recognition of leadership development and professional achievement for senior school leaders.

National Professional Qualification for Headship (NPQH)

This provides an opportunity for headteachers taking the next step to executive leadership with responsibility for more than one school.
