Testing the Continuing Professional Development (CPD) service

This programme allows you to experience the CPD service as a school induction tutor, lead provider or software vendor.

You can:

  • set up inductions for Early Career Teachers (ECTs) and mentors as a school
  • register for a National Professional Qualification (NPQ) as a teacher
  • manage a list of school partnerships
  • test teacher API integrations – including retrieval of lists of Early Career Framework (ECF) participants or NPQ applications and confirm an individual’s participation with a programme

Before you start

Testing as an ECF provider? You must contact us by email at continuing-professional-development@digital.education.gov.uk

We can then set up your test login.
