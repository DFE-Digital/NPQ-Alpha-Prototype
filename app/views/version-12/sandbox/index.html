Testing the Continuing Professional Development (CPD) service

This programme allows you to experience the CPD service as a school induction tutor, lead provider or software vendor.

You can:

  • setup inductions for Early Career Teachers (ECTs) and mentors as a school
  • register for a National Professional Qualification (NPQ) as a teacher
  • manage a list of school partnerships
  • test teacher API integrations – including retrieval of lists of ECF participants or NPQ applications and confirm an individual’s participation with a programme
