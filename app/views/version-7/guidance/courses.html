Available courses

From September 2021, NPQ courses previously will be changed.

These changes include:

  • replacing the existing NPQs in senior leadership, headship and executive leadership
  • replacing the current NPQ in middle leadership with 3 new NPQs for teachers and leaders who want to develop their expertise in specialist areas of teaching practice:
    • supporting the training and development of others
    • developing teachers who are subject leads or responsible for improving teaching practice in a subject or phase
    • developing teachers who have responsibilities for leading behaviour and culture
  • knowledge-based experience for the staffroom

These will be known as:

  • NPQ Senior Leadership
  • NPQ Executive Leadership
  • NPQ Headship
  • NPQ Leading Teaching Development
  • NPQ Leading Teaching
  • NPW Leading Behaviour and Culture
