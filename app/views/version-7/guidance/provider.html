Providers

There are nine training providers across the United Kingdom which run NPQ training courses.

They are:

  • Ambition Institute
  • Best Practice Network
  • Church of England
  • Education Development Trust
  • Harris Federation
  • Leadership Learning South East
  • Teacher Development Trust
  • Teach First
  • UCL Institute of Education

Some providers may have specific criteria depending on your school’s circumstances.

For example, the provider may require your school to be rated ‘good’ or ‘outstanding’ if you wish to train for their NPQ. Check with your current school to see if they recommended accredited partners for NPQ training.
